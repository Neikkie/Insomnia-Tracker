About the product

Built to make sleep patterns easier to understand.

Insomnia Trac is a sleep wellness app designed to help users track their nights, reflect on routines, and notice patterns over time through a calm, polished, privacy-conscious experience.

What Insomnia Trac does

The app gives users a simple way to record sleep hours, bedtime, wake time, mood, daily activities, notes, and habits like caffeine, alcohol, water intake, and late-night eating. It then organizes those entries into readable trends and history so the data feels useful instead of overwhelming.

When supported, Insomnia Trac also uses Apple Intelligence on device to generate a short weekly summary and one gentle next step. That keeps the product helpful while preserving a privacy-first approach.

Core product values

  • Clarity over clutter
  • Private on-device guidance when available
  • Simple logging that supports repeat use
  • Calm, trustworthy visual design
