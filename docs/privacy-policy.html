Privacy Policy

Your sleep data should feel personal and protected.

This page explains the current privacy approach for Insomnia Trac. Review it carefully and update it as needed to match your final App Store Connect disclosures and any future product changes.

Information storage

Insomnia Trac stores sleep logs on the user’s device. Entries may include sleep hours, sleep timing, notes, mood, routines, caffeine intake, alcohol intake, hydration, and related wellness details entered by the user.

How information is used

  • To display sleep trends, history, and summaries inside the app.
  • To generate in-app wellness insights based on user-entered patterns.
  • To support on-device AI summaries when Apple Intelligence is available.

AI and privacy

When supported, Insomnia Trac uses Apple Intelligence on device to generate short wellness summaries. The intended experience is privacy-forward and designed to keep user sleep information on the device where possible.
