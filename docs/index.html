The core product story

Sleep tracking apps often stop at raw logs. Insomnia Trac is built to help users understand what may actually be affecting their rest.

  • Log the context, not just the outcome. Sleep hours become more useful when paired with routine signals like caffeine, activity, alcohol, and late-night eating.
  • Turn data into reflection. Trends, history, and AI summaries help users spot patterns without overwhelming them.
  • Keep the experience calm. The visual language is intentional, night-sky inspired, and focused on trust rather than noise.
